<span id="_Toc6730" class="anchor"></span>I. Principle

1.1 Testing Principle:

DIC technology is a technical method to measure the strain and
deformation of the object surface. The method tracks the deformation
process of the scattering pattern on the surface of the object,
calculates the change of the gray value of the scattering domain, and
thus obtains the deformation and strain data of the surface of the
object under test.

1.2 The test follows the standard:

Meet the national standard JJG762-2007 0.5 level accuracy requirements.

1.3 Test range:

The test range: the field of view is better than or equal to 110mm.

<span id="_Toc28198" class="anchor"></span>Ⅲ. Equipment hardware
connection

3.1 Normal temperature test, through the test specimen to be tested on
the paste marking point, you can realize the software automatic
identification.

High-temperature test, the use of alcohol and high-temperature material
mixed evenly on the surface of the test specimen or high-temperature pen
directly on the surface of the test specimen, the production of
high-temperature marking points to complete.

3.2 According to different testing machines, select the appropriate
fixture, the specimen will be fixed in the tensile testing machine
fixture.

3.3 check whether the specimen installation in place, the first step
first to determine the equipment has been placed on the level and
distance between the correct, the second step to open the computer
software, turn on the camera, look at the field of view of the fixture
on the specimen is centered in the middle of the software field of view,
the image is presented clearly, whether the brightness of the light is
appropriate, all the adjustments are made, the equipment hardware ready
to complete.

3.4 Fixed tripod, head or mobile bracket.

3.5 Fix the quick release plate of the head on the bottom plate of the
2D extensometer.

3.6 Place the 2D extensometer on the tripod and adjust the relative
level and height.

3.7 According to the preset distance parameter of the 2D extensometer,
adjust the appropriate placement distance.

Example: Preset distance parameter 210mm.

1\) Measure the distance from the front edge of the device to the
specimen with a tape measure or length ruler, and adjust it to about
210mm. This completes the basic alignment of the 2D extensometer with
the center of the specimen to be tested.

2\) can use the equipment integrated laser ranging module, equipment
connection is complete, open the software, open the elongation meter
software interface in the upper right corner click on the laser ranging.

As shown in the figure: the red font 210mm for the current equipment
laser ranging irradiation to the distance of the sample surface, to
complete the elongation meter window and to be tested in the center of
the basic alignment of the sample. \[100mm\] is the software file
configuration when the laser ranging for 100mm, up to ± 2mm error when
the red font will change to green.

<img src="../../../../assets/products/video-extensometer/image10.png"
style="width:4.78611in;height:3.18194in" />

3.8 Light Source Connection: Connect the power cable to the light source
controller, and then connect the light source controller to the light
source through the black data cable.

3.9 Device connection: connect the data cable to connect the 2D
extensometer and the computer host.

3.10 Turn on the light source controller switch, the blue light source

3.11 Turn on the computer and perform software operation to fine-tune

<span id="_Toc26357" class="anchor"></span>VI. Maintenance of equipment
and precautions

6.1 Do not operate this instrument alone without professional training.

6.2 Avoid shining the light source to human eyes during use, which may
cause eye injury to the operator.

6.3 Under high temperature environment, try to wear high temperature
gloves to prevent burns, and pay attention not to stain the eyes when
making high temperature scattering or marking points.

6.4 When the instrument is not in use, it should be put into a box and
placed in a dry place, pay attention to shockproof, dustproof and
moisture proof.

6.5 The instrument should be transported in a box, the transportation
should be careful to avoid extrusion, collision and violent vibration,
long-distance transportation filled with software foam as a buffer.

6.6 Instruments installed to the tripod or dismantled, the first to hold
the instrument to prevent the instrument from falling.

6.7 Do not use chemical reagents to wipe the plastic parts and
plexiglass surfaces, can be used to wipe the soft cloth soaked in water.

6.8 Before measurement, the instrument should be carefully and
comprehensively checked to ensure that the indicators, functions and
power supply of the instrument meet the requirements before operation.

6.9 When the instrument functions abnormally, non-professional
maintenance personnel should not dismantle the instrument without
authorization to avoid unnecessary damages.
